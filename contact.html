FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll in any course by visiting our Courses page, selecting your desired course, and clicking "Enroll Now". Payment options are available during checkout.

Are the internships paid?

Most of our internship partners offer competitive compensation. Specific details about compensation are listed in each internship posting.

How does mentorship work?

Our mentorship program connects you with industry professionals for 1-on-1 sessions. You can book sessions directly through our platform and choose mentors based on your career goals.

What support is available for students?

We provide comprehensive support including career counseling, technical assistance, and access to our alumni network. Our student success team is available to help throughout your journey.
